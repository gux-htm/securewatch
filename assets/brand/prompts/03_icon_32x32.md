# Prompt: Icon — 32x32

**Use:** Browser favicon, window title bar icon, system tray icon, small UI badges

---

Recreate this exact logo as a **32x32 pixel icon**, heavily simplified for tiny display.

The design must be reduced to its most essential elements only:
- A bold shield silhouette with a thick cyan border — the shield shape must be immediately recognizable
- A white exclamation mark (!) centered inside — large enough to fill most of the shield interior
- A tiny padlock dot or minimal padlock hint at the very bottom of the shield (can be just a small circle if padlock detail is too small)
- NO circuit traces at this size — too small, will look like noise
- NO radar rings
- Strong contrast between the white ! and the dark shield body

Color palette:
- Shield body: dark navy #0A0F1E or #0D1B2A
- Shield border: bright cyan #00D4FF — must be thick enough to see at 32px
- Exclamation mark: pure white #FFFFFF
- Padlock hint: cyan dot #00D4FF

Style: pixel-perfect, maximum contrast, flat — no glow effects at this size. The icon must be instantly recognizable as a shield with an exclamation mark even at 32x32. Think favicon-level clarity.

Output: square canvas 32x32px, dark navy background, PNG format. Also provide ICO-compatible version.
